MY FAVORITE WWE SUPERSTARS!!!

These are the BEST wrestlers in WWE right now! They are super awesome and always have great matches! These are the top stars who make WWE so exciting to watch every week!!

John Cena

John Cena is the GREATEST OF ALL TIME!!! He has won 16 world championships which ties the record with Ric Flair! He is also a movie star now but still comes back to WWE sometimes!!!

Cena is known for his "Never Give Up" attitude and has granted more Make-A-Wish wishes than anyone else ever! He is super strong and can lift really big guys!

Finishing Move: Attitude Adjustment (AA) and the STF submission

Catchphrase: "You Can't See Me!"

Randy Orton

Randy Orton is called "The Viper" and he's one of the best wrestlers EVER!! He's a 14-time world champion and has been in WWE for over 20 years! He just came back from a really bad injury that almost ended his career!

Randy is a third-generation superstar because his dad and grandpa were also wrestlers! He's known for hitting the RKO "outta nowhere" which is the coolest finishing move in wrestling!

Finishing Move: RKO

Nickname: The Apex Predator, The Legend Killer

Seth "Freakin" Rollins

Seth Rollins is so AWESOME!! He has the best outfits and his laugh is really funny! He calls himself "The Visionary" and "The Revolutionary" and always puts on amazing matches!

Seth used to be in a group called The Shield with Roman Reigns and Dean Ambrose (now Jon Moxley in AEW). He's a multiple-time world champion and one of the best wrestlers technically in WWE!

Finishing Move: The Stomp

Catchphrase: "BURN IT DOWN!"

Cody Rhoades

Cody Rhodes is so INSPIRING!! His story is all about legacy, determination, and heart. He calls himself "The American Nightmare" and is known for his epic entrances, emotional promos, and unmatched passion for wrestling

Cody Rhodes is the son of Dusty Rhodes and brother of Dustin (Goldust). He helped build AEW, then returned to WWE to finish the story, becoming a fan favorite and Royal Rumble winner on his path to the WWE Championship.

Finishing Move: Cross Rhoades

Catchphrase: "Undesirable to Undeniable"

Roman Reigns

Roman Reigns is THE TRIBAL CHIEF and the HEAD OF THE TABLE!! He had the longest world championship reign in modern WWE history - over 1,300 days!!! That's more than 3 YEARS as champion!!

Roman is part of the famous Anoa'i wrestling family that includes The Rock, Yokozuna, Rikishi, The Usos, and Solo Sikoa! His story with The Bloodline is the best story in wrestling right now!

Finishing Move: The Spear and the Guillotine choke

Catchphrase: "Acknowledge Me!"

Bianca Belair

Bianca Belair is the EST of WWE - which means she's the strongEST, fastEST, toughEST, prettEST... you get it! She's AMAZING and so athletic! She can literally carry grown women on her shoulders while doing squats!

Bianca makes her own gear and uses her long braid as a weapon in matches! She won at WrestleMania twice and is one of the most popular women wrestlers ever!

Finishing Move: KOD (Kiss of Death)

Catchphrase: "I'm the EST of WWE!"

Rhea Ripley

Rhea Ripley is MAMI!!! She's super strong and intimidating but also really cool! She's part of The Judgment Day group and is one of the most dominant women wrestlers ever!

Rhea has an awesome punk rock look and can beat up men and women alike! She's from Australia and has held championships in NXT UK, NXT, and on the main roster!

Finishing Move: Riptide

Catchphrase: "This is MY Brutality!"

CM Punk

CM PUNK IS BACK!!! After leaving WWE for 10 years, he finally returned in 2023!!! This was the biggest surprise ever because everyone thought he would NEVER come back to WWE!

Punk is known as "The Best in the World" and "The Voice of the Voiceless" and gives the best promos in wrestling! He's controversial but super talented and the fans love him!

Finishing Move: GTS (Go To Sleep)

Famous Moment: The "Pipe Bomb" promo
