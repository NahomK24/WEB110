FUTURE WWE CHAMPIONS!!!

These wrestlers are going to be HUGE STARS really soon!!! They're super talented and are the future of WWE! Watch out for these awesome wrestlers because they're going to be champions soon!

Bron Breakker

Bron Breakker is SUPER ATHLETIC and SUPER STRONG!!! He's the son of Rick Steiner and nephew of Scott Steiner, so wrestling is in his blood! He was a football player before becoming a wrestler!

Bron was NXT Champion twice and is now on the main roster! He's really explosive in the ring and does an awesome spear and the Steiner Recliner like his dad and uncle! He's going to be world champion for sure!!!

Finishing Move: Spear and Steiner Recliner

Fun Fact: He runs the ropes at 23mph!

Tiffany Stratton

Tiffany Stratton is "The Center of the Universe" and she's AMAZING!!! She was a gymnast before WWE and does the PRETTIEST MOONSAULT EVER which is so cool!

She was NXT Women's Champion and now she's on SmackDown! Her character is like a rich, spoiled valley girl but she's actually super athletic and strong! She has star written all over her!

Finishing Move: Prettiest Moonsault Ever

Catchphrase: "Toodles!"

Ilja Dragunov

Ilja Dragunov is called "The Mad Dragon" and he's INTENSE!!! His matches are so physical and hard-hitting! He's from Russia but lives in Germany and was NXT UK Champion and NXT Champion!

Ilja has some of the BEST MATCHES EVER with guys like GUNTHER! He's not afraid of anything and will fight anyone no matter how big they are! His entrance is super cool with the way he moves his arms!

Finishing Move: H-Bomb/Torpedo Moscow

Nickname: The Unbesiegbar (The Unconquerable)

Carmelo Hayes

Carmelo Hayes is HIM!!! He's one of the coolest wrestlers in WWE with tons of swagger and amazing athletic ability! He was NXT North American Champion and NXT Champion!

Melo can do it all - high flying moves, technical wrestling, and talking on the mic! He's now on the main roster and is going to be a HUGE STAR! He has A+ confidence and ability!

Finishing Move: Nothing But Net (leg drop from the top rope)

Catchphrase: "I'm Him!"

Jacob Fatu

Jacob Fatu is the NEWEST MEMBER of The Bloodline and he's SCARY!!! He's related to Roman Reigns and The Usos as part of the famous Anoa'i wrestling family! He's so athletic it's UNBELIEVABLE!

Before coming to WWE, Jacob was a champion in MLW! He can do moonsaults and flips even though he's a big guy! He's going to make The Bloodline even more dangerous and will definitely be a champion soon!

Finishing Move: Moonsault and Samoan Drop

Fun Fact: He's the son of WWE legend Tonga Kid!
