CURRENT WWE CHAMPIONS!!!

These are all the awesome WWE Champions right now! Champions are the best wrestlers who have won their titles by beating other superstars in amazing matches! They have to defend their titles against challengers who want to become the new champions!

MEN'S CHAMPIONS

Jey Uso

World Heavyweight Champion

Jey Uso has stepped out from tag team success to capture singles gold! After years of tag team dominance with his brother Jimmy, Jey has proven himself as a solo competitor by winning the prestigious World Heavyweight Championship! YEET!

John Cena

Undisputed WWE Champion

The 16-time world champion has returned to break Ric Flair's record! John Cena captured his record-breaking 17th world championship by winning the Undisputed WWE Championship! YOU CAN'T SEE HIM!

Dominik Mysterio

Intercontinental Champion

The most hated man in WWE! Dominik has escaped from his father Rey's shadow and become a champion in his own right! With help from The Judgment Day, Dirty Dom captured the Intercontinental Championship!

Jacob Fatu

United States Champion

The newest member of The Bloodline has made an immediate impact by capturing the United States Championship! Jacob Fatu brings the same intensity and power that made his family famous in WWE!

WOMEN'S CHAMPIONS

Iyo Sky

Women's World Champion

The Genius of the Sky has soared to new heights as Women's World Champion! With her amazing aerial moves and technical wrestling skills, Iyo Sky has proven to be one of the most exciting champions in WWE!

Tiffany Stratton

Women's Champion

The Center of the Universe has captured gold! After an impressive run in NXT, Tiffany Stratton has brought her incredible athleticism and Tiffy Time to the main roster as Women's Champion!

Lyra Valkyria

Women's Intercontinental Champion

The Irish warrior has made history as the first-ever Women's Intercontinental Champion! Lyra Valkyria brings amazing high-flying moves and fighting spirit to this newly created championship!

Zelina Vega

Women's United States Champion

Don't let her size fool you! Zelina Vega has proven that heart and determination can overcome any obstacle! She's made history as the inaugural Women's United States Champion!

WHY CHAMPIONSHIPS MATTER!!!

Championships in WWE are super important! They show which wrestlers are the very best! Champions get:

  • More money and better contracts!
  • Main event matches at big shows!
  • Their names in the history books forever!
  • Awesome championship belts to carry around!
  • Respect from other wrestlers and fans!

The most prestigious championships are the World Heavyweight Championship and the Undisputed WWE Championship! These are the biggest prizes in WWE!

WWE has added new women's championships to give the amazing female superstars more opportunities to shine! Now there are women's versions of all the men's titles!
