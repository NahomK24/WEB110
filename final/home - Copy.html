WELCOME TO MY WWE WEBSITE!!!

Hi! Welcome to my WWE Today website! I made this for my web design class. World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) is the biggest wrestling company in the world!! They have shows every week and lots of awesome wrestling superstars!

Right now WWE is led by Triple H who is in charge of all the wrestling stuff since Vince McMahon left. The shows are way better now with more wrestling and cool stories that make sense!

WHAT WWE IS LIKE NOW

WWE has 3 main shows: Monday Night Raw, Friday Night SmackDown, and NXT. They also have big events every month that used to be called Pay-Per-Views but now they're called Premium Live Events. That's when all the best matches happen!!

WWE has lots of awesome wrestlers! Some are veterans who have been around a long time, and some are new wrestlers who are just getting started. They train at the WWE Performance Center in Orlando, Florida.

BIG NEWS: WWE is moving to Netflix in 2025!!! That means you'll be able to watch Raw on Netflix which is super cool!

WWE DOES A LOT OF STUFF

WWE isn't just wrestling! They do lots of other things too:

  • WWE Network is on Peacock now if you live in America
  • They tour all over the world with live shows
  • You can buy WWE toys and t-shirts in lots of stores

WWE is HUGE on social media with millions and millions of followers!! They post lots of videos and pictures to keep fans excited about wrestling!
